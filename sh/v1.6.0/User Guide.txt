Usage:
	Double Click script "LogAndroid2PC.bat" while DUT is ready to be test.
	Double Click script "StopLogging.bat" while DUT is finished test.
	Both StopLogging.bat and LogForTethering.bat can capture logs after tethering.
	LogForTethering.bat will be used by pc not support bat goto functions.
	logs store on folder:
		logs/androidlog_<datetoseconds>_<board>_<type>
